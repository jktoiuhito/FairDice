Privacy

This app runs purely on the memory of your browser. No cookies are used. No data is read from or stored to browser or device memory. No first- or third-party services, including the server serving this app, are contacted over the network during execution. As such, all signs of you ever using this app will be gone the moment you navigate away from or close the tab (or at the latest, close the browser) the app is running on.

The code for this app is served from servers managed by GitHub, which operates under its own privacy clause, according to which they might store some Personal Information from you, including your IP address.
