Privacy

FairDice

  • Does not communicate with any first- or third-party services over the network during its execution
  • Does not set or read any cookies
  • Does not store or read any data to or from your device
  • Does not in any extent enumerate the capabilities of your browser or device
  • Exists solely in the memory of your browser
    • Refreshing or closing the tab FairDice is running on permanently erases all of its data.

FairDice is free and open-source software. You can assert that the previous claims are true by examining the source code yourself.

The code for this app is served from servers managed by GitHub, which operate under their own privacy clause , according to which they might store some Personal Information from you, including your IP address.
